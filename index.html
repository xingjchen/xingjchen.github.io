About Me

Xingjian Chen | 陈行健

I am currently a postdoctoral research fellow co-advised by Prof. Jian Shu at MGH & Harvard Medical School and Prof. Collin M. Stultz at MIT. Recently, I obtained my Ph.D. from City University of Hong Kong under the supervision of Prof. Ka-Chun Wong. Earlier this year, I was a research intern in Computational Biology Group at Microsoft Research Asia under the supervision of Dr. Pan Deng. I am interested in Machine Learning and Bioinformatics. My current research focuses on the development of multimodal deep learning algorithms for multi-omics data integration (especially on Spatial Transcriptomics and Metagenomics). I am also interested in Biomedical Imaging and BioNLP.

Before arriving at CityU, I received my Master's Degree at Nanjing Agricultural University under the supervision of Prof. Wei Xue. Prior to that, I majored in pure wet-lab-based Biological Sciences:)
